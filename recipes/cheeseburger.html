Cheeseburger Recipe

Description

Time to sit back and relax with a cold one, and this juicy double stack cheeseburger!

Ingredients:

  • Ground beef burger patties
  • Cheese of your choice
  • Hamburger buns
  • Condiments of your choice

Steps to cook:

  • Place two patties onto the grill
  • While they begin to cook take your bun and toast them
  • After the burgers cook place cheese of choice onto the patties on grill
  • Take off the grill and put on bun with condiments
