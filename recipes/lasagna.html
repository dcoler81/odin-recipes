Lasagna Recipe

Description

Enjoy a warm and savory bite of this oven cooked three layer lasagna!

Ingredients:

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Noodles

Steps To Cook:

  1. Cook the meat
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Begin to layer the noodles down
  4. Pour sauce into pan with the meat
  5. Layer the lasagna and put in oven
